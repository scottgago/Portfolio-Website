About Me

I didn't start my career as a software developer. I fell into it my happenstance. I know it may sound cliche, but have you found that one thing in your life that keeps you awake at night because you're so excited by what it may bring you tomorrow?

For me, that love is software. There's something about software that brings the best out of me. It brings out of me creativity I never thought I had, passion I kept locked away for fear of ridicule, and a desire to be a part of something bigger than my individual efforts.

It brings out the artist in me that I never knew existed.

Software is my muse. My expression in this world that constantly changes and every night I go to sleep excited for what it will bring me tomorrow.
